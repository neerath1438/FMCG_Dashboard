FMCG Product Mastering
& 7-Eleven Gap Analysis

A comprehensive data intelligence solution to standardize, deduplicate, and analyze Nielsen FMCG product data across Peninsular Malaysia.

THE CHALLENGE

Why Product Mastering
Was Critical

⚠️

Duplicate Records

Same product sold under different UPCs, descriptions, and pack sizes — causing inflated SKU counts and incorrect sales aggregation.

🔀

Inconsistent Naming

"MUNCHYS OAT KRUNCH DARK CHOC 390G" vs "MUNCHYS OATKRUNCH DARK CHOCO 390GM" — same product, different entries.

📊

Gap Blindness

Without a clean master, it was impossible to accurately identify which Nielsen products are carried or missing in 7-Eleven stores.

Raw input had 7,192 Nielsen records with significant overlap, typos, and inconsistent formatting across brands.

OUR SOLUTION

A 2-Layer AI-Powered
Mastering Pipeline

1

Flow 1 — Rule-Based Strict Merge

Merges records with identical UPC + Market + MPack + Facts. Zero tolerance for incorrect grouping.

↓
2

Flow 2 — Agentic AI Attribute Extraction

Agentic AI extracts Brand, Product Line, Flavour, Variant, Size, and Product Form from raw descriptions.

↓
3

Hard Guards — Keyword Enforcement

Priority-ordered flavour & variant guards prevent over-merging. HAZELNUT ≠ CHOCOLATE CHIP.

🧠 LLM Prompt Design

  • Strict flavour distinction (CHIA SEED ≠ DARK CHOCOLATE)
  • Compound flavour preservation (STRAWBERRY & BLACKCURRANT)
  • Malaysian FMCG brand-aware examples
  • Typo normalization (MACADMIA → MACADAMIA)
  • Special variant detection (GOKUBOSO, MINI, FESTIVE)

⚡ Smart Caching

LLM results cached in MongoDB. Re-processing only hits API for new/changed items — saving cost and time.
