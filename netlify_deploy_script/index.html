📋 Presentation Script

FMCG Product Mastering & 7-Eleven Gap Analysis  |  Client: Roshini Alexander

Each slide — English script + Tamil explanation

Before you start: Open the presentation on your laptop. Keep this script on your phone or a printed copy. Speak slowly and clearly. After each slide, pause and ask "Any questions on this?"
1
🛒 Opening — Title Slide
FMCG Product Mastering & 7-Eleven Gap Analysis
🗣 Speak in English
"Good morning Roshini. Thank you for your time today. I'll be walking you through the FMCG Product Mastering project — what problem we solved, how our system works, and the final results and deliverables."
💬 Tamil Explanation
இந்த slide-ல் project title மட்டும் இருக்கு. Confident-ஆ smile பண்ணி பேசுங்கள். "walk you through" என்றால் "step by step காட்டுவேன்" என்று அர்த்தம். Nervous ஆகாதீர்கள் — இது simple opening!
2
⚠️ The Challenge
Why Product Mastering Was Critical
🗣 Speak in English
"The core problem was inconsistent product data. The same product appeared under different names and barcodes in the Nielsen dataset — causing inflated SKU counts and incorrect sales figures. For example, 'Munchys Oat Krunch Dark Choc 390G' and 'Munchys OatKrunch Dark Chocolate 390GM' are the exact same product — but they showed up as two completely separate records. Without fixing this, it was impossible to do an accurate comparison with 7-Eleven data."
💬 Tamil Explanation
"Same product, different names-la irunthuchu" என்று சொல்லுங்கள். Screen-ல் Oat Krunch example point பண்ணுங்கள். "SKU count" என்றால் unique product count. "Inflated" என்றால் actual-ஐ விட அதிகமாக கண்டதுனு சொல்லுங்கள்.
3
🤖 Our Solution
A 2-Layer AI-Powered Mastering Pipeline
🗣 Speak in English
"To solve this, we built a 2-layer AI pipeline. The first layer uses strict rules — if two records share the same barcode and market, they're merged automatically. The second layer uses Agentic AI to read each product description and extract key attributes — Brand, Flavour, Size, and Variant — and then intelligently group similar products together. On top of this, we have built-in safety guards that prevent similar-sounding but actually different products from being incorrectly merged — for example, Hazelnut cookies will never be merged with Chocolate Chip cookies."
💬 Tamil Explanation
"2 step process — first rules-based, then AI" என்று சொல்லுங்கள். "Safety guards" என்பது Hazelnut-ஐ Chocolate Chip-உடன் merge பண்ணாம பார்க்கும் logic. Left side flow steps-ஐ point பண்ணி explain பண்ணுங்கள். Right side-ல் LLM Prompt Design card இருக்கு — "these are the rules we gave the AI" என்று சொல்லுங்கள்.
💡 Tip
Client technical details கேட்டால்: "We use enterprise-grade AI with a custom prompt designed specifically for Malaysian FMCG product naming patterns." — இந்த one line போதும்.
4
📊 Key Results
The Numbers Speak
🗣 Speak in English
"Here are the key numbers from this project. We processed all 7,192 Nielsen records. We also analysed 3,220 products from 7-Eleven's catalogue. Most importantly, we identified and successfully merged 107 product groups where duplicate records existed — consolidating them into clean, single master records. Our AI extraction maintained consistently high confidence across all records."
💬 Tamil Explanation
Screen-ல் 4 numbers இருக்கும் — ஒவ்வொன்னையும் point பண்ணி சொல்லுங்கள். "107 product groups" — "இந்த 107 groups-ல் same product different names-ல் irunthuchu, athai oru single record-a pannom" என்று சொல்லுங்கள்.
5
✅ Accuracy Proof
Before vs After — Oat Krunch
🗣 Speak in English
"Let me show you a concrete example of what this looks like. Before our system, all Oat Krunch products — regardless of flavour — were being grouped into a single record. Chia Seed, Dark Chocolate, Strawberry and Blackcurrant, Nutty Choco — all treated as the same product. That's clearly wrong. After our fix, each flavour now has its own dedicated cluster. The AI correctly separates them based on the flavour attributes it extracts from the product name."
💬 Tamil Explanation
Left side = "Before" (wrong), Right side = "After" (correct). Left-ஐ point பண்ணி "Before — everything was one cluster" சொல்லுங்கள். Right-ஐ point பண்ணி "After — each flavour is now separate" சொல்லுங்கள். Visual-ஆ clearly தெரியும் — client புரிஞ்சுக்குவாங்க.
6
🔒 Skinny Baker Fix
Priority-Based Flavour Guard
🗣 Speak in English
"Another challenge was handling typos and abbreviations in product names. Our system automatically corrects these. For example, 'MACADMIA' — a spelling mistake in the raw data — is correctly mapped to 'MACADAMIA'. 'CRNBRIES' becomes 'CRANBERRY'. 'HZLNT' becomes 'HAZELNUT'. This is done using a priority-based guard system — it checks for specific flavours first, before falling back to generic ones. This ensures products like Hazelnut Chocolate Chip and Double Chocolate Chip are kept as separate groups."
💬 Tamil Explanation
Right side-ல் Skinny Baker examples இருக்கு — ஒவ்வொன்னையும் point பண்ணுங்கள். "Typo normalization" — "Raw data-ல் spelling mistake irunthalum namma system correct-a identify panum" என்று சொல்லுங்கள். Bottom-ல் "* Typo normalization" note இருக்கு — அதை client கவனிக்க வைக்கலாம்.
7
🏪 7-Eleven Gap Analysis
Nielsen vs 7-Eleven: What's Missing?
🗣 Speak in English
"Now for the gap analysis — this is where we compare Nielsen's full product range against what's available in 7-Eleven stores. Out of 7,192 Nielsen products, we found that 910 are currently carried in 7-Eleven. That leaves 6,282 Nielsen products that are NOT in 7-Eleven — these are the gap products. We used a 3-level matching approach: first we match by exact barcode, then by cluster membership, and finally by brand and attribute similarity with a 10% size tolerance."
💬 Tamil Explanation
"6,282 gap products" — "இந்த products Nielsen-ல இருக்கு, but 7-Eleven-ல இல்ல" என்று சொல்லுங்கள். "Potential opportunities" — "7-Eleven இந்த products stock பண்ணா customer demand meet பண்ண முடியும்" என்று சொல்லலாம். Table-ல் L1, L2, L3 matching levels இருக்கு — "we have 3 ways to find a match" என்று சொல்லுங்கள்.
💡 High Impact Moment
இந்த slide-ல் client most interested ஆவாங்க. Slowly explain பண்ணுங்கள். கேள்வி கேட்டால் "The full list is in the Excel report — 7Eleven_Not_Carried_Items.xlsx" என்று சொல்லுங்கள்.
8
📁 Deliverables
6 Final Reports Ready to Use

Six Excel reports — including the complete Master Stock with all enriched attributes, the combined Gap Analysis report showing all Nielsen products with their 7-Eleven status, separate reports for carried and not-carried items, and the full raw datasets for both Nielsen and 7-Eleven. All files are structured in the exact column format you requested. Everything is ready for your team to use immediately."
💬 Tamil Explanation
ஒவ்வொரு file-ஐயும் point பண்ணி பேசுங்கள். "7Eleven_Gap_Analysis.xlsx — இது combined report, carried + not-carried எல்லாம் இதுல இருக்கு" என்று சொல்லுங்கள். "Everything is ready for your team to use immediately" — இந்த last sentence confident-ஆ சொல்லுங்கள். பிறகு "Do you have any questions?" என்று கேளுங்கள்.
💡 Closing
Presentation முடிஞ்சவுடன்: "Thank you Roshini. All the files have been shared. Please let me know if you need any changes or additional analysis." — இந்த ஒரு line சொல்லி முடிங்கள்.
